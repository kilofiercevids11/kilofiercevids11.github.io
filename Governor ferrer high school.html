Background of School.

We are a Government Public Highschool that focus on giving a proper and well education for our students.

Our School "Governor Ferrer Memorial National Highschool - Biclatan Annex" is a public highschool that offers best education that base on the standard education system under the Department of Education. Our mission is to teach our students a great education to guide them on choosing and making a decision when they grow up. We have many subjects that can help our students to improve their skills in learning such as Mathematics, English, Filipino, Science and more. We also help the physical health of our students in our P.E subject. Every year, many students from other places are transferring or planned to attend in our school. Our schools have many various events too that can help the confidence of our students.
